Meta-Analysis
"A coordinate-based meta-analysis of music-evoked emotions" : Published in 2020, this meta-analysis synthesizes 47 neuroimaging studies with 944 subjects, using activation likelihood estimation (ALE). Key findings include:
Large clusters in amygdala, anterior hippocampus, auditory cortex, and reward network structures (ventral/dorsal striatum, anterior cingulate cortex, orbitofrontal cortex, secondary somatosensory cortex).
Highlights music's rewarding nature, the auditory cortex as an emotional hub, and the hippocampus's role in attachment and social bonding.
This provides neurological evidence for the framework's mapping, with a large sample size meeting the ≥100 participant criterion.
