## Executive Summary

FeelX is a revolutionary music discovery platform that answers the fundamental question: **"What makes this song feel X?"** - where X could be sophisticated, euphoric, melancholic, or any emotional response. By combining 400+ years of music theory with cutting-edge AI research and leveraging Spotify's technical infrastructure, FeelX creates personalized "Musical DNA" profiles that help users rediscover their emotional connection to music.

Unlike algorithmic recommendation systems that focus on popularity or similarity, FeelX analyzes the **emotional architecture** of music through modal analysis, chord progression mapping, and cross-cultural emotional research to deliver unprecedented personalization and discovery experiences.
